Why the same payment gets you out faster

Manulife One is an offset mortgage — your income deposits reduce your balance daily, so every dollar you earn is working against your debt the moment it arrives.

1

Your paycheque deposits directly into your mortgage account

Unlike a traditional mortgage where income sits idle in a chequing account, your deposits immediately reduce your outstanding balance — cutting interest from day one.

2

Interest is calculated on your daily balance — not a fixed number

Traditional mortgages charge interest on a static balance. Manulife One charges interest on what you actually owe today. With income sitting in the account, that number is lower — often by thousands of dollars per year.

3

Your monthly surplus pays down principal automatically

Whatever is left after expenses stays in the account — no extra transfers, no manual steps. That surplus relentlessly chips away at the principal, year after year, shaving years off your amortization.

4

Fixed portions available for clients who want stability

You can lock part of the balance into a fixed-rate sub-account if rate certainty matters. The rest stays flexible and continues benefiting from the offset mechanism.
