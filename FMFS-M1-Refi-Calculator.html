Why this beats standard M1:
The HELOC/Hybrid strategy applies your surplus as lump-sum prepayments to the fixed term — which typically carries a lower rate than M1 (4.95%). By doing the sweep, you're paying down cheaper-rate debt faster while keeping the offset working on the LOC side.

The rule: When your LOC balance approaches $0, trigger the sweep. Waiting too long means the account goes positive — and that positive balance earns nothing.

M1 vs. HELOC — Key Difference

M1Automatic daily offset. No manual sweeps needed. All-in-one account. Rate: 4.95%
HELOC / HybridManual sweep required every ~6 months. More effort, but lower rate on fixed portion saves more total interest.
